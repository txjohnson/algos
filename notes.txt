Play area -- grid 5 x 6
Need a list of 5 letter words
Keyboard for letters and then 2 commands, enter and backspace
Once user entered 5 letters
    Enter submits the guess
    Backspace takes away one letter

When a guess is submitted
    The tiles change to indicate correct, wrong, semicorrect
    Keyboard is colored that way as well

Every wrong guess, moves down 1 row

GAme is won when player guess the words
GAme is lost when there are no more rows
